Winner by Category

Best Value: Jackery Explorer 1000 v2

At $999 with 1,070 Wh capacity, Jackery offers the best price-per-capacity ratio among the top three. The 22 lbs weight is a major advantage if you need to move your power station around. The 4,000-cycle LiFePO4 battery means it'll last over a decade of weekly use.

Best Overall: EcoFlow Delta 2

EcoFlow edges ahead with its blazing-fast X-Stream charging (50 minutes to 80%) and the ability to add an extra battery for 2,048 Wh total. The 5-year warranty is also the best in class. If you can afford the extra $100, the Delta 2 is the most versatile home backup.

Best Capacity: Bluetti AC180

The Bluetti AC180 offers the most capacity under $1,000 at 1,152 Wh. It charges nearly as fast as the EcoFlow and features the longest-lasting battery (4,000+ cycles). The trade-off is weight — at 37 lbs, it's 15 lbs heavier than the Jackery.

Recommendation by Use Case

Apartment dwellers: Go with the Jackery 1000 v2. It's light enough to move from closet to living room, and its quieter fan makes it bedroom-friendly during overnight use.

Homeowners (small to medium home): The EcoFlow Delta 2 is your best bet. Fast charging and expandability give you flexibility for longer outages.

Extended outages (2+ days): Bluetti AC180 or EcoFlow Delta 2 + extra battery. The extra capacity makes a real difference when the power's out for days.

Final Verdict

There's no wrong choice here — all three are excellent. But if we had to pick one for most people, it's the EcoFlow Delta 2. It balances capacity, speed, expandability, and build quality better than any other option. The 5-year warranty is the cherry on top.
